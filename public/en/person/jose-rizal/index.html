José Rizal

호세 리살
Writer, Nationalist, Physician · 1861–1896

Life

  1. 1861Born on 19 June in Calamba, Laguna, the seventh child of a prosperous landowning family.source
  2. 1882Left for Madrid to study medicine, specializing in ophthalmology at the University of Madrid.source
  3. 1887Published his first novel, Noli me tángere, in Berlin, exposing the abuses of Spanish colonial rule.source
  4. 1891Published the sequel El filibusterismo in Ghent, Belgium, cementing his role as a leading reform spokesman.source
  5. 1892Returned to the Philippines and founded the nonviolent reform society La Liga Filipina; was soon deported into exile at Dapitan, Mindanao.source
  6. 1896Tried for sedition by the military and executed by firing squad by Spanish colonial authorities in Manila on 30 December.source
