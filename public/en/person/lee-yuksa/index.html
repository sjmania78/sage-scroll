Life

  1. 1904Born in Wonchon-ri, Dosan-myeon, Andong, Gyeongbuk. Real name Yi Won-roksource
  2. 1925Joined the Uiyeoldan together with his older and younger brotherssource
  3. 1927Imprisoned at Daegu Prison for involvement in the bombing of the Daegu branch of the Bank of Joseon (prisoner number 264, the origin of his pen name 'Yuksa')source
  4. 1939Published the poem 'Green Grapes' (Cheongpodo) in Munjangsource
  5. 1940Published the poem 'The Summit' (Jeoljeong) in Munjangsource
  6. 1943Arrested in Seoul in the autumn and transported to Beijingsource
  7. 1944Died for his country in a Beijing prison in Januarysource
  8. 1945His posthumous poem 'The Wilderness' (Gwangya) was published in the Jayu Sinmun by his younger brother Yi Won-jo (December 17)source
