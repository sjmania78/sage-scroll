Life

  1. 1910Born in Banjeong-dong, Sunhwa-bang, Gyeongseong (September 23). Real name Kim Hae-gyeong (金海卿), Gangneung clansource
  2. 1913Entered and grew up in the home of his uncle Kim Yeon-pil (Tongin-dong)source
  3. 1926Graduated from Donggwang School (later merged into Boseong High Common School)source
  4. 1929Graduated from the architecture department of Gyeongseong Higher Technical School. Appointed as an engineer in the architecture section of the Internal Affairs Bureau of the Government-General of Koreasource
  5. 1933Resigned his post as Government-General engineer and ran tearooms and cafes. Published the poem 'Mirror'source
  6. 1934Joined the Guinhoe (Group of Nine). Serialized the poem cycle 'Crow's-Eye View' (Ogamdo) in the Joseon Jungang Ilbo (discontinued after 15 poems)source
  7. 1936Published the short story 'Wings' in the September issue of Jogwang. The same year, went to Tokyo, Japansource
  8. 1937Detained in Tokyo, then released on bail for illness; died at the hospital attached to Tokyo Imperial University (April 17, at age 27)source
