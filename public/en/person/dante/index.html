Life

  1. 1265Born in Florence into a family of lesser nobility.source
  2. 1274At age nine he first met Beatrice Portinari, who became central to his poetry.source
  3. 1294Completed 'La Vita Nuova' dedicated to Beatrice, following her death in 1290.source
  4. 1300Elected one of the priors who governed Florence.source
  5. 1302Exiled from Florence after the defeat of the White Guelphs; he never returned.source
  6. 1308Began writing his masterpiece, the 'Divine Comedy', during exile.source
  7. 1321Died of malaria in Ravenna, where he was buried.source
