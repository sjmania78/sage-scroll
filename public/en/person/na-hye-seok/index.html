Na Hye-seok

나혜석
Western-style painter and writer · 1896–1948

Life

  1. 1896Born in Suwon, Gyeonggi Province (April 28, 1896)source
  2. 1913After graduating from Jinmyeong Girls' High School, entered the Western painting department of the Joshibi (Women's School of Fine Arts) in Tokyo, Japansource
  3. 1918Published the short story 'Gyeonghui' in the magazine Yeojagye; graduated from the Women's School of Fine Arts the same yearsource
  4. 1920Married the lawyer Kim Woo-yeongsource
  5. 1921Held a solo oil-painting exhibition at the Naecheonggak hall of the Gyeongseong Ilbo companysource
  6. 1922Began submitting to the Western painting section of the Joseon Art Exhibition (Seonjeon), winning selections and special prizes over the following yearssource
  7. 1927Took a round-the-world tour of Europe and America with her husband, staying about eight months in Parissource
  8. 1929Held a 'European-American Sketch Painting Exhibition' in Suwonsource
  9. 1930Divorced Kim Woo-yeongsource
  10. 1934Published 'A Confession of Divorce' in the magazine Samcheollisource
  11. 1935Held a 'Recent Small Works Exhibition' at the Joseongwan exhibition hall in Seoul (her final exhibition)source
  12. 1948Died at the Seoul Municipal Jajewon (Jahyewon) shelter (December 10, 1948)source
  13. 2000Na Hye-seok Street created in Ingye-dong, Paldal-gu, Suwonsource
