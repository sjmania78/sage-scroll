Sin Chae-ho

신채호
Historian, journalist, and independence activist · 1880–1936
Explore places on the map

Life

  1. 1880Born in Sannae, Daedeok, South Chungcheong Province, and raised in Cheongju, North Chungcheong Province. Pen name Danjae (丹齋); ancestral seat Goryeongsource
  2. 1906Appointed editor-in-chief of the 'Daehan Maeil Sinbo'source
  3. 1908Published 'Doksa sillon' (serialized in the Daehan Maeil Sinbo)source
  4. 1910Went into exile in Chinasource
  5. 1923Wrote and issued the 'Joseon Revolution Declaration' for the Uiyeoldan (Heroic Corps)source
  6. 1928Arrested by Japanese police in Keelung, Taiwansource
  7. 1931'Joseon Sanggosa' was serialized in the arts and culture section of the 'Chosun Ilbo'source
  8. 1936Died in Lushun Prison (cerebral hemorrhage, aged 57)source

Works

Joseon Sanggosa (Ancient History of Korea) 1931

An account of ancient history. Serialized in the 'Chosun Ilbo' in 1931 and published as a book in 1948. The first chapter (general introduction) sets out his theory of history.
