Rabindranath Tagore

라빈드라나트 타고르
Poet, Writer, Philosopher, Composer · 1861–1941
Explore places on the map

Life

  1. 1861Born into the Tagore family at Jorasanko, Calcutta (Kolkata).source
  2. 1910Published the Bengali poetry collection Gitanjali (4 August).source
  3. 1913Awarded the Nobel Prize in Literature for Gitanjali; the first non-European laureate.source
  4. 1919Renounced his knighthood in a letter (30 May) in protest of the Jallianwala Bagh massacre.source
  5. 1921Founded Visva-Bharati University at Santiniketan.source
  6. 1929Composed the poem 'Lamp of the East' for Korea, published in the Dong-A Ilbo.source
  7. 1941Died in Calcutta (Kolkata) on 7 August.source

Works

Gitanjali

A Bengali poetry collection (1910); its English version, Song Offerings (1912), brought him the 1913 Nobel Prize in Literature.
