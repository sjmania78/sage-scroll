Life

  1. 1545Born in Geoncheon-dong, Hanseong (near present-day Inhyeon-dong, Jung-gu, Seoul) (March 8 by the lunar calendar). Ancestral seat Deoksu, courtesy name Yeohaesource
  2. 1576Passed the regular military examination and entered government servicesource
  3. 1591Appointed Naval Commander of the Left Jeolla Province (Jeolla Jwasusa)source
  4. 1592Outbreak of the Imjin War. Engaged the Japanese navy at the Battle of Okpo and otherssource
  5. 1592Naval battle off Hansando (7th lunar month, the Great Victory at Hansando)source
  6. 1593Appointed Commander-in-Chief of the Three Provinces' Navy (Samdo Sugun Tongjesa); established his headquarters at Hansandosource
  7. 1597Dismissed from office and taken in custody to Hanseong, then served as a common soldier (baeguijonggun)source
  8. 1597Naval battle at Myeongnyang (Uldolmok, between Jindo and Haenam) (September 16 by the lunar calendar)source
  9. 1598Killed in action during the naval battle at Noryang (Namhae, South Gyeongsang) (November 19 by the lunar calendar). Aged 54source
  10. 1614His tomb was moved to its present location at Eorasan in Eumbong-myeon, Asansource

Works

Nanjung Ilgi (War Diary) 1592

A handwritten diary kept over the seven years of the Imjin War (1592-1598). National Treasure, held at Hyeonchungsa Shrine. Inscribed on the UNESCO Memory of the World Register in 2013.
