An Jung-geun

안중근
Independence activist and thinker · 1879–1910

Life

  5. 1909Shot Itō Hirobumi at Harbin Station (October 26)source

Works

Calligraphy 'Gyeolli Saui Gyeonwi Sumyeong (見利思義見危授命)' 1910

A calligraphic work written in Lushun Prison. It bears the inscription 'Written by An Jung-geun, a Korean, in Lushun Prison, third month of the Gyeongsul year (1910)' and an imprint of his left palm.

Treatise on Peace in East Asia (東洋平和論) 1910

An unfinished essay written in Lushun Prison. Only the preface and the 'Jeongam' (historical survey) sections were completed.

Verify source

An Eung-chil Yeoksa (安應七歷史, The Autobiography of An Eung-chil) 1910

An autobiography written in prison (December 1909 - March 1910). The original does not survive and is transmitted in manuscript copies.
