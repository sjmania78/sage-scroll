Western-style painter · 1914–1965

Life

  1. 1914Born in Jeongnim-ri, Yanggu-myeon, Yanggu-gun, Gangwon Province (February 21, 1914)source
  2. 1932Selected at the Joseon Art Exhibition (Seonjeon) with the watercolor 'Spring Comes'source
  3. 1936Continued to submit works to the Joseon Art Exhibition through 1944source
  4. 1952During the Korean War, fled south and settled in Seoul, beginning to submit works to the Korean Fine Arts Association Exhibition and the National Art Exhibitionsource
  5. 1959Became a recommended artist of the National Art Exhibition (Gukjeon)source
  6. 1962Served as a judge of the National Art Exhibitionsource
  7. 1965Died in Jeonnong-dong, Dongdaemun-gu, Seoul (May 6, 1965)source
  8. 2002The Yanggu County Park Soo-keun Museum opened on the site of his birthplace in Jeongnim-ri, Yanggu-eup (October 25, 2002)source
