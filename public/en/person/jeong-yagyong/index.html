Jeong Yak-yong

정약용
Silhak scholar and government official · 1762–1836
Explore places on the map

Life

  1. 1762Born in Majae, Chobu-myeon, Gwangju-bu, Gyeonggi Province (present-day Neungnae-ri, Joan-myeon, Namyangju)source
  2. 1783Passed the jinsa (licentiate) examinationsource
  3. 1789Passed the singnyeon mungwa civil service examination in the first class (gapgwa); began his official career as an officer at Heungneung (Huireung) Tombsource
  4. 1789Completed the pontoon bridge (jugyo) over the Han Riversource
  5. 1793Designed Suwon Hwaseong Fortresssource
  6. 1801Arrested in the Catholic Persecution of 1801 (Sinyu Persecution) and exiled to Janggi in Gyeongsang Province; in the same year, transferred into exile in Gangjin, Jeolla Provincesource
  7. 1817Wrote 'Gyeongse yupyo' during his exile in Gangjinsource
  8. 1818Completed 'Mongmin simseo' during his exile in Gangjinsource
  9. 1818Released from exile after 18 years and returned home to Majaesource
  10. 1836Died at his home (Yeoyudang) in his native Majaesource
