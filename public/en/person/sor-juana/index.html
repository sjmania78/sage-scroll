Life

  1. 1648Born in San Miguel Nepantla, New Spain (per baptismal record; some sources cite 1651)source
  2. 1664Moved to Mexico City and served at the viceregal court as lady-in-waiting to Vicereine Leonor Carretosource
  3. 1669Entered the Hieronymite convent of Santa Paula (San Jerónimo), where she remained for lifesource
  4. 1690Her 'Carta Atenagórica' was published; the Bishop of Puebla rebuked her under the name 'Sor Filotea'source
  5. 1691Wrote 'Respuesta a Sor Filotea de la Cruz', defending women's right to educationsource
  6. 1695Died on 17 April in Mexico City after caring for fellow nuns during an epidemicsource
