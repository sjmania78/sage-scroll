Sejong the Great

세종대왕
4th King of Joseon · 1397–1450
Explore places on the map

Life

  1. 1397Born as the third son in King Taejong's private residence in Junsubang, Hanseong (April 10 by the lunar calendar). Given name Yi Dosource
  2. 1418After being invested as crown prince, received the throne abdicated by Taejong and was enthroned at Geunjeongjeon Hall of Gyeongbokgung Palacesource
  3. 1420Established the Jiphyeonjeon (Hall of Worthies)source
  4. 1434Installed the Jagyeongnu (self-striking water clock) and cast the Gabinja metal movable typesource
  5. 1441Produced the Cheugugi (rain gauge) and the water gaugesource
  6. 1443Created the 28 letters of Hunminjeongeum (12th lunar month)source
  7. 1446Promulgated Hunminjeongeum (9th lunar month). The Haerye (explanatory) edition was publishedsource
  8. 1450Passed away in Hanseong after 31 years on the throne (February 17 by the lunar calendar). Temple name Sejongsource
  9. 1469His tomb was relocated to Yeoju, Gyeonggi. Tomb name Yeongneungsource
