Life

  1. 1564Born in Stratford-upon-Avon and baptized at Holy Trinity Church on April 26 (birthday traditionally celebrated April 23).source
  2. 1582Married Anne Hathaway (marriage bond dated November 28, 1582).source
  3. 1592First documented in London as an established actor and playwright.source
  4. 1594Became a founding member and shareholder of the playing company the Lord Chamberlain's Men.source
  5. 1599His company built the Globe Theatre in Southwark on the south bank of the Thames, in which he held a share.source
  6. 1616Died on April 23 in Stratford-upon-Avon and was buried at Holy Trinity Church.source
  7. 1623The First Folio, the first collected edition of his plays, was published seven years after his death.source
