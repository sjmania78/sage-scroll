Life

  1. 1916Born in Songcheon-ri, Joun-myeon, Pyeongwon-gun, South Pyeongan Province (April 10, 1916)source
  2. 1936After graduating from Osan Higher Common School, entered the Western painting department of the Imperial Art School in Tokyo, Japansource
  3. 1937Transferred to the art department of Bunka Gakuin in Tokyosource
  4. 1940Graduated from Bunka Gakuinsource
  5. 1945Married the Japanese woman Yamamoto Masako (Yi Nam-deok) in Wonsansource
  6. 1950During the Korean War, left Wonsan and took refuge on Jeju Island by way of Busansource
  7. 1951In January 1951, took refuge with his family in Seogwi-dong, Seogwipo, residing there for about a yearsource
  8. 1952Moved to Busan, then lived an itinerant life moving to Tongyeong and other placessource
  9. 1955Held a solo exhibition at the Midopa Department Store gallery in Seoulsource
  10. 1955Held a solo exhibition in Daegusource
  11. 1956Died at the Red Cross Hospital in Seodaemun, Seoul (September 6, 1956)source
