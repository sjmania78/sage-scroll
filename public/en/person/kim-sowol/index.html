Kim So-wol

김소월
Poet · 1902–1934
Explore places on the map

Life

  1. 1902Born in Guseong, Pyeonganbuk-do (real name Kim Jeong-sik)source
  2. 1920Made his literary debut publishing 'Spring of a Wanderer' (Nangineui Bom) and other works in the magazine Changjosource
  3. 1922Entered Baejae High Common School. Published 'Azaleas,' 'Golden Lawn,' 'Mother, Sister' and others in Gaebyeoksource
  4. 1923Entered the preparatory course of Tokyo University of Commerce in Japan; withdrew and returned home due to the Great Kanto Earthquakesource
  5. 1925Published the poetry collection 'Azaleas' through Maemunsa. Published the poetics essay 'The Soul of Poetry' (Sihon) in Gaebyeoksource
  6. 1934Died in Gwaksan, Pyeonganbuk-dosource

Works

Azaleas (Jindallaekkot) 1925

The only poetry collection published in his lifetime, issued by Maemunsa in 1925. The title poem was first published in Gaebyeok in 1922, a free-verse poem of 4 stanzas and 12 lines.
