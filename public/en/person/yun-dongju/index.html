Life

  1. 1917Born in Mingdong village, Bukgando (December 30). Childhood name Haehwan (海煥), Papyeong clansource
  2. 1935Transferred to Sungsil Middle School in Pyongyangsource
  3. 1938Entered the literature department of Yeonhui College in Seoulsource
  4. 1941Graduated from Yeonhui College. Wrote 'Prologue' (Seosi, November 20) and 'Night of Counting Stars'source
  5. 1942Studied in Japan. Entered Rikkyo University in Tokyo, then transferred to the English literature department of Doshisha University in Kyotosource
  6. 1943Arrested in Kyoto on charges of violating the Peace Preservation Law (July 14), sentenced to 2 years in prisonsource
  7. 1945Died in prison at Fukuoka Prison, Japan (February 16, at age 27)source
  8. 1948Posthumous poetry collection 'Sky, Wind, Stars, and Poetry' (Haneulgwa Baramgwa Byeolgwa Si) published (Jeongeumsa). First edition with 31 poemssource
