Life

Works

Sehando (Winter Scene) 1844

A painting made for his pupil Yi Sang-jeok during his exile on Jeju. Its colophon quotes the Analects passage that only after the cold sets in does one know the pine and cypress do not wither. National Treasure.

Yedang Geumseok Gwaanrok (Yedang's Record of Epigraphy Examined)

A work of epigraphy. It verified that the Bukhansan stele and the Hwangchoryeong stele were monuments commemorating King Jinheung's tours of inspection (the Bukhansan stele verified in 1816).
