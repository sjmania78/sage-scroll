Life

  1. 1907Born in Bongpyeong, Pyeongchang, Gangwon Provincesource
  2. 1925His poem 'Spring' was selected as an honorable-mention work in the Maeil Sinbo New Year literary contestsource
  3. 1925Graduated from Gyeongseong First High School and entered Keijo Imperial Universitysource
  4. 1928Published the short story 'The City and the Ghost' (literary debut)source
  5. 1930Graduated from the English literature department, College of Law and Letters, Keijo Imperial Universitysource
  6. 1933Joined the Guinhoe (Group of Nine) and published the short story 'Don (The Pig)'source
  7. 1934Appointed professor at Soongsil College in Pyongyangsource
  8. 1936Published 'When the Buckwheat Blooms' in the October issue of Chogwang (publishing 'Mountain' and 'Field' the same year)source
  9. 1939Published the novel 'Pollen' and the short stories 'Yeosu' and 'The Emperor'source
  10. 1940His wife Lee Gyeong-won died; published the novel 'Boundless Blue Sky'source
  11. 1942Died of meningitis in Pyongyang (aged 36)source
