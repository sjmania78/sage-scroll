Raden Adjeng Kartini

라덴 아젱 카르티니
Pioneer of women's education and writer · 1879–1904

Life

  1. 1879Born 21 April into a Javanese noble family; her father later became Regent of Jeparasource
  2. 1885From about age six attended a Dutch-language primary school, becoming fluent in Dutchsource
  3. 1899On 25 May wrote her first letter to Dutch friend Stella Zeehandelaar, beginning her correspondencesource
  4. 1903On 8 November married the Regent of Rembangsource
  5. 1904Gave birth to a son on 13 September and died at Rembang on 17 September, aged 25source
  6. 1911J.H. Abendanon compiled and published her letters in Dutch as 'Door Duisternis tot Licht'source
  7. 1964Declared a National Hero of Indonesia; her birthday, 21 April, is observed as Kartini Daysource
