Life

  1. 1828Born into an aristocratic family at the Yasnaya Polyana estate in Tula Governorate, Russia.source
  2. 1855Served as an artillery officer in the siege of Sevastopol (1854–55) during the Crimean War.source
  3. 1862Married Sophia Behrs.source
  4. 1869Completed the novel 'War and Peace', written at Yasnaya Polyana from 1862.source
  5. 1878Published 'Anna Karenina' in book form (serialized 1875–1877).source
  6. 1901Was excommunicated by the Russian Orthodox Church.source
  7. 1910Died at Astapovo railway station after leaving Yasnaya Polyana, and was buried in the estate's forest.source
