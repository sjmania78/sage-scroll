Mark Twain

마크 트웨인
Novelist · 1835–1910
Explore places on the map

Life

  1. 1835Born Samuel Langhorne Clemens on November 30 in Florida, Missouri, in the year Halley's Comet passed Earth.source
  2. 1839At age four, moved with his family to Hannibal, Missouri, on the Mississippi River, the setting for his fiction.source
  3. 1859Became a licensed Mississippi River steamboat pilot.source
  4. 1863First used the pen name 'Mark Twain', a riverman's term for water two fathoms (about 3.7 m) deep.source
  5. 1876Published The Adventures of Tom Sawyer.source
  6. 1885Adventures of Huckleberry Finn published in the United States.source
  7. 1910Died of a heart attack on April 21 in Redding, Connecticut, as Halley's Comet again passed Earth.source
