Works

Seonghak Sipdo (聖學十圖, Ten Diagrams on Sage Learning) 1568

A Neo-Confucian textbook presented to King Seonjo in 1568, composed of ten illustrated diagrams with explanations.

A sequence of twelve sijo poems composed in 1565. The first six express one's aspirations (eonji), and the latter six express learning (eonhak).
